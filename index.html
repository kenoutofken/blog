Hi! My name is Ken!

Welcome to my humble little web page. I am a Designer, Photographer, and Front-End Developer based out of Vancouver, BC! Originally I am from Edmonton, AB, and I am currently a student at Wilson School of Design to expand my skills in web and interactive application development. Every day has been a new experience for me so I thought it would be great to create a space for me to gather my own thoughts as I continue this journey towards the next chapter of my life. On my website you will find my blog which contains:

  • Thoughts and reflections of my experiences here in my new home.
  • Photography from my adventures!
  • Examples of my front-end development and design work.
  • Links to some design and creative related websites for inspiration and reference.

If you are interested, you can also check out my full photography portfolio at www.kenoutofken.com! I'm always looking to collaborate with other creatives, so don't hesitate to reach out! Thanks for visiting!
